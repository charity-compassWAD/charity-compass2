The floods in Kenya

Heavy rains and floods continue to wreak havoc across Kenya, resulting in loss of lives, property, and service disruption. Transport, housing, education, health, and agriculture are the most affected sectors by the floods, according to the authorities. An estimated 267 people have been killed, 188 injured, 75 missing, while 281,835 people (56,367 families) have been displaced and 380,573 (76,114 families) affected by persistent heavy rains and flooding as of 9 May, according to the National Disaster Operations Centre (NDOC).
